ABOUT

Our most important milestones.

  • 2016

    KOREEZ STUDIO FOUNDED

    We are 6 people team who all love playing games and we hope that you'll enjoy the ones created by us, too. We develop games in Unity and applications in iOS and Android native languages.

  • SEPTEMBER 2016

    PARALLYZED AND ROOFBOT LAUNCHED

    Our platformer hit Parallyzed was among winners of Google Play Indie Game festival!

  • OCTOBER 2016

    WORLDWIDE FEATURING

    Both games got appraisal both from players and reviewers. Featured on launch on the App Store, Roofbot became Apple's Free app of the week several months later.

  • JULY 2016

    ROOFBOT INSTANT

    HTML5 market is booming! And we launched our first effort in Facebook Instant games this November!
